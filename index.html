ABOUT
Hi! I’m Kennice Mascarenhas, a first-year design student at Atlas Skilltech University with a deep-rooted passion
for creativity, music, and nature. My journey in the arts began in high school, where I developed my skills in
Art & Design courses and discovered the magic of multimedia storytelling.Beyond design, I’m inspired by the
wonders of nature and the thrill of adventure. I love capturing the beauty of landscapes, wildlife, and candid moments during treks and explorations through my photography. Spending time outdoors fuels my creativity, while my connection with pets adds a touch of joy and warmth to my everyday life. Music is another vital part of my world. I sing, play a few instruments, and immerse myself in diverse genres that spark my imagination and enhance my creative process.Although I’m an introvert at heart, I thrive in collaborative settings, contributing fresh ideas and a thoughtful approach to teamwork. My artistic journey is one of constant exploration, spanning intricate illustrations, dynamic designs, and expressive photography.This website is a reflection of my evolving skills, passions, and dedication to the creative process. Welcome to my world of art, music, and design!
